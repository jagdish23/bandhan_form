GENUINE WEBSITE IDENTIFICATION

Genuine website identification is extremely important as it prevents inadvertent sharing of personal credentials over a seemingly genuine, but fake website set up by fraudsters.

IDENTIFY A GENUINE WEBSITE BY FOLLOWING THESE STEPS

STEP 1

LOOK FOR URL WITH 'HTTPS' AND LOCK ON

1. Secure Sockets Layer(SSL) sertificates secure the transfer of your crucial data
2. Genuine website is easily identified by the lock symbol
3. Also look for 'https' instead of 'http' as a prefix to the website address

STEP 2

CHECK THE DOMAIN NAME
